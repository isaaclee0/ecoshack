#### Cabin and Bathroom

This week we completed this little garden studio in Tranmere, prefabricated in the Ranelagh workshop. Delivered flatpacked, assembled on site over three days. 3x3 studio, 1.5x3m deck, clad in Woodland Grey corrugated iron and Tas. Oak, double glazed window and doors, fully insulated, lined with 12mm ply and included 240 volt plug and play power. (Power can be adapted for off-grid).
